## Gig Description

**AI is not a gimmick anymore -- it is infrastructure.** I help startups and businesses integrate large language models (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini) into their applications with code that belongs in production, not a tutorial.

### What I Build

I deliver AI integrations that are reliable, cost-efficient, and maintainable:

- **Chatbots and conversational interfaces** -- Customer support bots, internal knowledge assistants, AI copilots embedded in your product
- **RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)** -- Your AI answers questions using YOUR data: PDFs, documentation, databases, websites. No hallucinations about information that exists in your knowledge base.
- **Document processing** -- Extract structured data from invoices, contracts, forms, and emails using LLMs
- **Content generation pipelines** -- Blog posts, product descriptions, email campaigns, social media content generated at scale with human-quality output
- **Sentiment analysis and classification** -- Analyze customer feedback, reviews, support tickets, and social mentions automatically
- **AI-powered search** -- Semantic search that understands meaning, not just keywords
- **Multi-model routing** -- Use GPT-4 for complex reasoning, Claude for long documents, Gemini for multimodal tasks. Automatic fallback if one provider is down.

### Real Projects I Have Built

These are not hypotheticals. These shipped to production:

- **AT-Function-GPT** -- Built a GPT-4o-mini powered system that analyzes support tickets, classifies them by urgency and department, extracts key entities, and routes them to the right team. Reduced manual triage time by 70%.
- **Obenan Sentiment Engine** -- Integrated AI-based sentiment analysis into a multi-tenant SaaS platform. The system processes thousands of customer reviews across 100+ business locations, identifying trends and flagging negative sentiment in real time.
- **GoTo API Transcription** -- Architected an AI transcription pipeline using NestJS that processes audio recordings, generates accurate transcripts, and extracts action items and summaries. Handles concurrent processing with BullMQ job queues.

### Why Production-Grade Matters

Most AI integrations on Fiverr are thin wrappers around the OpenAI API. They work in demos but break in production. Here is what I do differently:

- **Streaming responses** -- Users see tokens appear in real time instead of waiting 10+ seconds for a wall of text
- **Error handling** -- Graceful fallbacks when the AI provider has downtime (it happens more than you think)
- **Cost controls** -- Token tracking, budget limits, and smart caching so your AI bill does not spiral
- **Prompt versioning** -- Manage and A/B test prompts without redeploying code
- **Security** -- Input sanitization to prevent prompt injection attacks

## FAQ

**Q: Which AI model should I use -- GPT-4, Claude, or Gemini?**
A: It depends on your use case. GPT-4 excels at reasoning and code. Claude handles long documents (200K tokens) and nuanced instructions. Gemini is strong at multimodal tasks (images + text). I will recommend the right model after understanding your requirements.

**Q: How much will the AI API costs be after you build it?**
A: I design every integration with cost efficiency in mind. For a typical chatbot handling 1,000 conversations per day, expect $50-200/month in API costs depending on the model. I include token tracking and budget alerts so there are no surprises.

**Q: Can you integrate AI into my existing application?**
A: Absolutely. Most of my projects involve adding AI features to existing apps. I work with any Node.js, Python, or REST-compatible backend. Send me your tech stack and I will confirm compatibility.

**Q: What is RAG and do I need it?**
A: RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) lets the AI answer questions using your specific data -- documents, databases, websites -- instead of just its training data. If you want the AI to know about YOUR business, products, or content, you need RAG. It is included in the Premium package.

**Q: Can the AI handle multiple languages?**
A: Yes. GPT-4, Claude, and Gemini all support multilingual input and output. I can configure the system to detect the user's language and respond accordingly.

**Q: Do you offer ongoing maintenance for AI integrations?**
A: Yes. AI models get updated frequently and prompts may need tuning as your data changes. I offer monthly maintenance packages starting at $100/month.
